About Me [Curriculum Vitae]

I am a machine learning researcher at Apple AI/ML Information Intelligence.

I obtained my PhD degree from University of Southern California with Prof. Fei Sha and Prof. Leana Golubchik.

Previously, I was a research intern & student researcher at Google Brain, Google Research Language, AWS Rekognition, and Tencent AI lab at Seattle. I was a visiting student at SIAT MMLab.
