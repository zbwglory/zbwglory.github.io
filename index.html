About Me (CV)

I am a PhD student with Prof. Fei Sha in the TEDS lab of Department of Computer Science at University of Southern California.

Previously, I obtained my M.Eng. and B.Eng. degree from Tongji University. My advisor is Prof. Hanli Wang. I was a visiting student of Multimedia Research Center at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology under the supervision of Prof. Yu Qiao.
