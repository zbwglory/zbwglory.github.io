About Me (CV)

I am a PhD student in Department of Computer Science at University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Fei Sha.

I obtained my M.Eng. and B.Eng. degree from Tongji University. My advisor is Prof. Hanli Wang. Previously, I was a visiting student of Multimedia Research Center at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology under the supervision of Prof. Yu Qiao. I am fortunate to work closely with Dr. Limin Wang and Zhe Wang.
